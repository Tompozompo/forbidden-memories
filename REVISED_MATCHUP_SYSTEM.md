# Revised 10-Type Combat Style System

## Final Proposed System: "Quake" with Improved Shadow Matchups

### The Problem
- **Shadow beating Frost** doesn't make sense - they're both cold/dark themed
- Creates thematic redundancy
- **Shadow beating Bolt** feels forced

### The Solution
- **Shadow beats Impact + Venom** instead
- Makes Shadow more about "intangibility" - darkness as immaterial force
- Physical force (Impact) can't hit what has no substance
- Darkness absorbs/hides poison (Venom flows into shadow)

### What Changed
1. **Shadow** no longer beats Bolt + Frost
2. **Shadow** now beats Impact + Venom
